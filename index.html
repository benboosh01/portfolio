Ben Bushell

Junior Software Developer

In 2022 after 11 years as a sales advisor/manager I decided to take the jump and make my dream of becoming a software developer a reality.

I found my passion for coding when my sales department needed detailed information on our sales performance. I took it upon myself to learn excel VBA in my own time so I could provide that information to front line employees and senior leaders, allowing us to spot opportunities to improve our performance. This ignited a spark inside me, and I decided to start learning software development through various resources such as freeCodeCamp, team treehouse and YouTube.

The opportunity then presented itself to join Northcoders on a 3-month bootcamp program and I could not resist. Now a Northcoders graduate I am ready to take the next step and begin my career as a software developer.

I would love to find a company where I can continue to grow my acquired skillset as well as demonstrate my dedication to being the best I can be.
